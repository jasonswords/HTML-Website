Jay's Hobby Blog

Hello and welcome to my hobby blog. I am a computing student at the institute of technology, Blanchardstown. I have a wide variety of interests, including Shooting, RC Aeroplanes, Rc Helicopters, Drones, Motorcycles and of coarse, Technology. I was asked to produce a blog of my own choosing and design. After some careful thought, I choose to blog about my hobbies. I have many hobbies which I enjoy immensely but I will be concentrating on motorcycles and on RC technology which is a very fun and interesting hobby. RC airplanes, helicopters, and drones are extremely interesting in their own right and have many different characteristics and challenges. I will discuss these challenges in more detail in each individual page relating to the discipline at hand. On this blog, i will be talking about my motorcycle and the various RC models i own myself and have experience with.

Have a look around and I hope you find something that may be of interest to you. If any further information is needed, please use the social media contacts at the bottom of the page.

Jason Swords
